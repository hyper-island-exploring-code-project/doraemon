Grilled fish over charcoal fire, this is a classic in a delicacy. There are many ways to prepare grilled fish, but charcoal-grilled fish is a method with unique charm.

The history of charcoal grilled fish can be traced back to ancient times. In some areas of ancient China, people liked to use charcoal fire to grill fish food, and this grilling method has gradually evolved into a traditional grilled fish method.
